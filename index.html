Hey! I'm Jack, and I design tabletop RPGs.

I do so in the spare minutes of my day; sat on a train, walking along a busy street, rocking my baby to sleep, and so on. It's a strangely disjointed way of working, but if I'm finding my way back to a design over & over it's usually a good sign.

Most designs rightfully end up in the composter, but a few make it all the way and end up published. I hope you enjoy checking them out!
